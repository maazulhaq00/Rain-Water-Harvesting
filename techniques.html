Rainwater Harvesting Techniques

Explore different techniques for effective rainwater harvesting.

Rooftop Rainwater Harvesting

Collect rainwater from rooftops, utilizing gutters, downspouts, and storage tanks for various purposes, promoting water self-sufficiency and conservation.

Learn More
Surface Water harvesting

Capture rain runoff using various techniques to manage and utilize surface water for agricultural, environmental, and domestic purposes.

Learn More
Ground Water
Recharge

Replenish underground aquifers through intentional water infiltration, fostering sustainable water resources and supporting ecosystems and communities.

Green Roof Systems

Implementing vegetation on rooftops to absorb rainwater and reduce runoff.

Learn More
Permeable Pavements

Using porous materials for pavements to enable water infiltration and recharge groundwater.

Learn More
Rain Gardens

Learn More
Artificial Wetlands

Building wetland areas to mimic natural processes, enhancing water filtration and storage.
